Welcome to Space Balls!

Game Controls:
- Use the keypad to guide your orb. Rotate it with the left and right keys, press up to move forward.
- Press the 'f' key to fire a flare and locate the flag.
- Press the 'shift' key to boost your orb and travel faster.

How to Win:
- Hit the flag to change it to your team's color.
- Defend the flag from the other team, to prevent them changing it to their color.
- Whatever color the flag is when the timer runs out, that team wins the round!
- There may be other ways to win too - you'll have to play to find out!
